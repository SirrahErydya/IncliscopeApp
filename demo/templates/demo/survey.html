Welcome to Incliscope!

The bayesian framework Incliscope is a novel way to estimate inclinations of galaxies based on optical images. Instead of fitting ellipsoids, we use a probabilistic approach to collect properly-calibrated posterior distributions among inclinations from simulated galaxies and use them as training data for a Deep Convolutional Mixture Density Network (DCMDN).
